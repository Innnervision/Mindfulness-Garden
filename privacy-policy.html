📋1. Introduction

Welcome to PranaVerse: Mindfulness ("the App"), developed and operated by Universe Invedors ("we", "us", or "our"). The App is a wellness application that provides guided meditation, breathing exercises, yoga sessions, healing frequencies, and a mindfulness garden experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

This policy complies with the Google Play User Data Policy, GDPR, and CCPA.

📊2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address — provided when registering with email/password or Google Sign-In
  • Display name — your chosen profile name
  • Profile avatar — emoji or image you select within the app

Account creation is optional. Core features work without signing in.

2.2 Health and Wellness Information

To personalise your experience, you may optionally provide:

  • Age group
  • Wellness goals (e.g., reduce stress, improve sleep)
  • Self-reported health conditions (e.g., anxiety, insomnia)
  • Daily mood logs
  • Meditation session history and duration
  • Breathing exercise records
  • Streak and progress data
This information is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to your private Firebase Firestore account. We do not sell or share health data with advertisers.

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version (Android)
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (for analytics and ad personalisation)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information

2.4 Analytics Data

  • Screens viewed and features used
  • Session frequency and duration
  • In-app events (e.g., exercise started, garden planted)
  • App performance metrics

2.5 Local Storage

The App stores preferences, settings, and offline data locally on your device using Hive (local database) and SharedPreferences. This data does not leave your device unless you are signed in and sync is enabled.

2.6 Notifications

If you grant notification permission, we store your reminder preferences locally to deliver scheduled meditation and breathing reminders. We do not use push notifications for marketing without your consent.

🎯3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App's features
  • Personalise your wellness recommendations and AI coach responses
  • Sync your progress across devices when you are signed in
  • Send optional local notifications for meditation reminders
  • Detect and fix crashes and technical issues
  • Measure feature usage to prioritise improvements
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Process in-app subscription purchases through Google Play and RevenueCat
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do not use your health or wellness data for targeted advertising. Ad personalisation is based only on general device identifiers via AdMob.

🤝5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Firebase, AdMob, and RevenueCat as described above — only as necessary to provide the App's features
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: If we merge with or are acquired by another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before any such transfer
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Universe Invedors, our users, or the public
Advertising through Google AdMob may involve the use of device identifiers for ad personalisation. You can opt out of personalised ads in your Android device settings under Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

🔒6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Firebase encrypts data stored on their servers at rest
  • Local data: Data stored on your device is protected by Android's built-in security sandbox
  • Access control: Firebase security rules restrict database access to authenticated users only
  • Crash monitoring: Firebase Crashlytics monitors for security-related crashes

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us at universeinvedors@gmail.com.

⏱️7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Session history & progress: Retained for as long as your account is active, or until you request deletion
  • Crash logs: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase Analytics for up to 14 months (configurable)
  • Local device data: Retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data
  • Ad interaction data: Managed by Google AdMob per their retention policy

After account deletion, we will remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days. Some anonymised aggregate data may be retained for analytics purposes.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

GDPR rights (EU/EEA users)

CCPA rights (California users)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at universeinvedors@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

🗑️9. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data through two methods:

In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open PranaVerse: Mindfulness
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Your account and all cloud-synced data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

By Email

Send a deletion request to universeinvedors@gmail.com with subject line: "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account.

Deleting your account does not affect locally stored data on your device. To remove local data, uninstall the app or go to Android Settings → Apps → PranaVerse → Clear Data.

👶10. Children's Privacy

PranaVerse: Mindfulness is intended for users aged 13 and older. The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at universeinvedors@gmail.com and we will promptly delete that information.

Users between 13 and 17 should use the App with parental supervision. The premium subscription requires a valid payment method, which minors typically do not possess without parental involvement.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these jurisdictions in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
